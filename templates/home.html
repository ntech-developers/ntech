Ntech Hackathon 2019

N-Tech (New Technologists & Innovators) is a youth-led initiative that aims to empower youth to drive technological advancements as the fourth Industrial Revolution unveils. Young people today are going to be the leaders of tomorrow. But, is it not mismatching that one third of the, rapidly accelerating, youth population in Africa is unemployed today?
N-Tech hackathon is an annual event and we are currently seeking Participants (undergraduate or graduate university students) and sponsors for this year's event. They will team-up depending on topics that they are mutually interested in, work together for 30 hours to develop a solution to the prompt. Thereafter, submissions will be evaluated by a panel of judges and the selected finalists will pitch their ideas to the organizations. Top teams will then be selected.

Currently involving three countries

This year, 2019, it will concurrently be hosted in Kenya, Rwanda, and Uganda (we hope to expand in the future). Each country will have a theme that relates to some of the major issues but still contributes to our overall mission, empowering youth to take the lead in the fourth industrial revolution.
